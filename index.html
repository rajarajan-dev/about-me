About DailyDose

Welcome to DailyDose, your go-to app for managing medications and staying on top of your health.

With DailyDose, you can:

  • Create and manage your medications.
  • Filter and organize your pills.
  • Manually track your medication intake.

Our mission is to make medication management simple and effective, helping you stay healthy and organized.
